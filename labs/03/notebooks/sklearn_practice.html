World Cup Challenge

In this notebook you’ll apply what you’ve learned about scikit-learn to a real-world question: can we predict match outcomes from World Cup stats?

You’ll complete two exercises: - Exercise 1 — Regression: predict how many goals a team scores from their shots on target - Exercise 2 — Classification: predict whether a team advances to the knockout stage

The Dataset

Each row is a team’s group-stage performance from recent World Cup tournaments. - shots_on_target — shots that required the goalkeeper to make a save - possession_pct — share of time the team controlled the ball - goals_scored — total goals in the group stage - advanced — 1 if the team reached the knockout round, 0 if eliminated

Run the cell to load the data:
